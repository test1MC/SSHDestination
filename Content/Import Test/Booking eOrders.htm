Booking eOrders

Electronic orders (eOrders) and Manual Orders (see Building a Schedule) can be booked in OTPIX.

A seamless process allows users to process eOrders and send back order changes to the agency without leaving OPTIX.

  1. Open the BOOK queue and follow the guided workflow within the platform.
  2. Identify the Order and Choose an Action.
  1. Click EDIT or PROCESS

OR

  1. Right-click on line item

   

Upon right clicking on the line item, the following options are available. A subset of these options are available when selecting the from the menu bar.

 

  1. Once complete, click Process then follow workflow prompts.
  1. View Order Details to review information that will be included in the agency buysheet pdf.
  2. Details That Require Your Attention must be completed to continue with the booking process.
  1. Select Sales Coordinator.
  2. Select Survey Book.
  3. View Conditions of Sale: these are pre-populated per Agency defaults.

 

 

  1. Add Condition of Sales (COS) settings for Agencies that don’t have defaults.
  2. For Political Agencies, the view will be slightly altered.
  1. Once selected, TRANSFORM adds the ability to adjust RATES and Flight Start/Flight End dates.

  1. Optional steps
  1. Click Details (A) to add a Product or Buyer Name.
  2. Click Back Office (B) to add AvailPro # or change Order Type.
  3. Select dropdown (C) to access system Details (i.e. print), Notes and eFolder
  4. Click Next to open order in Scheduler.

 

  1. View Scheduler and Transmit
  1. Review Scheduler Details.
  2. Review totals by syscode by selecting “Summary” from the Toolbar.

  1. Quickly add Comment to order – Selecting Bookends will display comments only on lines with a 15s spot duration.

  1. Click Export for a printable Excel version.

Note: File will be automatically saved to eFolder when transmitted through OPTIX.

  1. Click Transmit to send to MVPD/MySPOT.
  1. Choose Default System Comments from the lower-left corner of the Comment screen or enter custom system comments.

Note: Use Ctl+Click to select all systems for Default comments.

A confirmation email will be sent to the AE and System Coordinator once the order successfully transmits.
