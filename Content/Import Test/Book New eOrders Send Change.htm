Book New eOrders & Send Change to Agency Prior to Transmit

Follow the process for booking orders from the OPTIX BOOK queue. see Booking eOrders.

  1. Click Process then follow workflow prompts.
  1. Details That Require Your Attention must be completed to continue with the booking process.
  2. Select Sales Coordinator.
  3. Select Survey Book.
  4. View Conditions of Sale: these are pre-populated per Agency defaults.
  1. Add COS settings for Agencies that don’t have defaults.
  1. Click Next to open order in Scheduler.
  1. View Scheduler and Send Makegood

Lines that need to be changed prior to transmitting will be outlined in red. Hover text will indicate what the issue is.

  1. Revise order in Scheduler as needed.
  1. Ex. Remove spots from existent lines – add new lines as needed.

 

 

  1. Save as Order – Only Saved Drafts can be sent as Makegood Offers.
  2. Send order change to Agency.

 

Things to know:

When the agency approves the offer the system order will be transmitted to the MPVD.  In the event the order has other syscodes that did not require changes, the order can be accessed in the book queue to be edited and transmitted.

The Makegood Form will auto-capture into the eFolder.

Transmitting the schedule without sending a makegood may result in the order being out of sync with the agency.
