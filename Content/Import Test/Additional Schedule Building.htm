Additional Schedule Building Tools:

Presto

  1. Rate cards data can quickly be added to the Scheduler lines by selecting Presto from the ribbon.
  2. Current rate cards will be highlighted. Simply check the rate card and select APPLY.
  3. The rate card Rate and Source will pull into the scheduler in unique columns. 
  4. The rate can then be compared to the existing scheduler rate. 
  1. Use Ctrl-c / Ctrl-v to copy/paste the rates to the rate column, if desired.
  1. As new lines are entered into the Scheduler, the rate card rate and source will populate.

 

 

Send Electronic Makegood Offer to Agency

  1. Load Scheduler and select Open.
  1. Enter search criteria.

 

  1. Orders will display based on search criteria entered. 
  2. Choose the Order and select LOAD to launch into Scheduler.

  1. Schedule details will load.
  2. Customize the schedule by utilizing the tools.
  1. Hide Columns
  2. Sort
  3. Filters

 

 

Tracker

Easy to track changes in Spots, GRPs and Dollars on Orders.

 

  • Tracker can be located by selecting Summary from the ribbon.
  • Click the down-arrow to see one syscode instead of all of them.
  • Totals are adjusted based on the syscode selected.
  • Ordered: Last transmitted totals.
  • Proposed MG: Automatically adjusts as you add/remove spots.
  • Change: Keeps track of uneven changes. Color codes change to green (+) or red (-);

e.g. if 2x removed on a line, the Change column reflects - 2 on the Spots row indicating the order is now -2x than the last transmitted amount.

  • Duplicated lines will display under the line selected for duplication.
  • New lines are highlighted in green.

 

Save as Order

Message will display indicating that order has been saved.

 

eOrders:

Once order has been saved, select “Send” to send electronic Makegood offer to agency.

 

 

Things to know:
  • Select Recall from the ribbon to pull Makegood offer back from an agency.
  • This process may take up to 15 minutes to complete.
  • Revert will take the order back to the last transmitted version.
  • Electronic makegoods can be sent for DARE and Strata orders.  Review the header back office to determine the source type.
  • Manual Orders:
  1. Follow procedures to notify agency buyer of makegood offer.
  2. A message will display once offer is sending to the agency. 
  3. Once the buyer approves the makegood offer the system order will transmit to the MVPD.
